# Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We pledge to make participation in the ForgeCLI community a respectful, inclusive, and harassment-free experience for everyone.

ForgeCLI is built to simplify DevOps workflows, deployment automation, and infrastructure management. We welcome contributors of all backgrounds and experience levels.

## Our Standards

Examples of positive behavior include:

* Being respectful and professional.
* Providing constructive feedback.
* Helping new contributors learn and grow.
* Collaborating openly and transparently.
* Sharing knowledge and best practices.
* Supporting the project and, if you find it useful, considering giving the repository a ⭐ star.

Examples of unacceptable behavior include:

* Harassment, discrimination, or hateful conduct.
* Personal attacks, trolling, or intimidation.
* Publishing private information without permission.
* Deliberately introducing malicious or insecure code.
* Disruptive behavior that negatively impacts the community.

## Security and Responsible Development

Because ForgeCLI interacts with deployment pipelines, infrastructure, and cloud environments, contributors should:

* Follow secure coding practices.
* Never expose secrets, credentials, or tokens.
* Report vulnerabilities responsibly.
* Prioritize reliability, stability, and security.

## Enforcement Responsibilities

Project maintainers are responsible for enforcing community standards and may remove content or restrict participation when necessary.

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to all project spaces, including issues, pull requests, discussions, documentation, and community communication channels.

## Enforcement

Instances of unacceptable behavior may be reported to project maintainers through official project channels.
